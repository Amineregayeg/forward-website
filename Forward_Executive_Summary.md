## The Three Prompts: Solutions Delivered

### **Prompt 1: Best-Practice Landing Page Anatomy**
✅ **Delivered: Complete 9-Section Framework**

**Recommended Section Order (Top-to-Bottom):**
1. Hero Section (Problem-focused headline + soft CTA)
2. Social Proof & Trust Signals (logos, certifications, stats)
3. Problem Section (pain points in prospect language)
4. Methodology (3–5 core principles + benefits)
5. Results/Snapshots (2–3 case study cards with metrics)
6. Risk Mitigation Roadmap (hidden costs, specific mitigations)
7. Deep-Dive Case Study (one detailed example + quote)
8. Credentials & Differentiators (certifications, experience, partnerships)
9. Final CTA (primary + secondary option + privacy reassurance)

**Key Differentiators for Regulated Industries:**
- Lead with governance-first architecture (not features)
- Show audit-ready timelines (Day 1, not 6 months post-launch)
- Emphasize senior expert continuity (vs. Big 4 team rotation)
- Provide specific risk mitigation strategies
- Quantify results: timeline compression, data quality %, team independence

### **Prompt 3: French/EU Enterprise Tone & Microcopy**

✅ **Delivered: Complete Tone Guide + CTA Examples + Do's/Don'ts**

**Tone Framework: Adaptive Formalism**

| **Touchpoint** | **Formality Level** | **Example** |
|---|---|---|
| **Discovery** | Warm, professional | "Découvrez comment nous simplifions les migrations complexes" |
| **Proposal/Contracts** | Formal, precise | "La gouvernance des données sera établie selon le cadre standard Salesforce..." |
| **Post-Sale** | Partnership-oriented | "Votre équipe continuera à bénéficier de notre soutien..." |

**Recommended CTA Language (French):**
- Primary: **"Réservez un atelier de découverte"** (Book discovery workshop)
- Alternative: **"Planifiez votre diagnostic de gouvernance"** (Plan governance assessment)
- Secondary: **"Téléchargez : Guide SFMC pour Secteur Santé"** (Download: SFMC guide for healthcare)
- Avoid: "Parlez à un expert" (generic), "Demandez une démo" (premature)

**Microcopy Examples:**
- Form: *"Vos informations sont confidentielles. Temps de réponse : 24h"*
- Compliance badge: *"Conforme RGPD | ISO 27001 certifiée"*
- Testimonial: *"Noms changés pour confidentialité. Métriques vérifiées."*

**Do's & Don'ts:**
- ✅ DO: Name specific frameworks, regulations, quantified results
- ❌ DON'T: Use jargon without explanation or make unquantified claims
- ✅ DO: Show understanding of buyer's constraints
- ❌ DON'T: Sound like a generic agency; avoid superlatives

## French/EU Copywriting Quick Rules

**✅ DO:**
- Specific frameworks & methodologies
- Quantified results & metrics
- Show understanding of buyer constraints
- Role-specific language (IT, Marketing, Compliance, Finance different angles)
- Transparent about trade-offs (e.g., "Compliance rigor takes 3-4 weeks upfront but saves 6 months post-launch")

**❌ DON'T:**
- Jargon without explanation
- Unquantified superlatives ("Revolutionary," "Leading-edge")
- Sound like a generic agency ("Nous offrons des services de classe mondiale")
- Use overly casual French
- Make unverifiable claims

**CTA Tone:**
- Consultative: "Échangeons sur votre feuille de route"
- Diagnostic: "Planifiez votre diagnostic de gouvernance"
- Discovery: "Réservez un atelier de découverte"
- Educational: "Découvrez les bonnes pratiques en 5 minutes"

**Avoid:**
- "Parlez à un expert" (generic, sales-y)
- "Demandez une démo" (only if they're ready for product walkthrough)
- "Commencez maintenant" (creates false urgency)
